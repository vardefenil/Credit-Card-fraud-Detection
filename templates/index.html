🧠 How It Works

1

Data Collection

284,807 real credit card transactions with 30 features (PCA-transformed for privacy).

2

SMOTE Balancing

Synthetic oversampling handles the extreme class imbalance (0.17% fraud rate).

3

Multi-Model Training

4 algorithms compete: Logistic Regression, Random Forest, XGBoost, and LightGBM.

4

Real-Time Prediction

The best model is deployed for instant fraud probability scoring.
